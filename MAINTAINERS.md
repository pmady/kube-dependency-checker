## Becoming a Maintainer

To become a maintainer, you should:

1. Have a track record of contributions to the project
2. Demonstrate understanding of the project's goals and architecture
3. Show commitment to the project's long-term success
4. Be nominated by an existing maintainer
5. Be approved by the majority of current maintainers

## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review and merge pull requests
- Triage issues
- Participate in project discussions
- Help grow the community
- Follow and enforce the Code of Conduct
